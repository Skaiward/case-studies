# Legal AI Document Generation Platform

**Domain:** Legal Technology
**Challenge:** Democratize access to legal assistance through AI-powered document analysis and generation
**Outcome:** Court-compliant legal documents generated in minutes with deadline automation

---

## Overview

Built a comprehensive legal technology platform combining AI document analysis, intelligent questionnaires, and professional legal document generation for eviction defense and employment law matters. The system extracts key information from uploaded legal documents, guides users through relevant questions, and produces court-formatted filings.

## Key Achievements

- **Automated Document Intelligence:** OCR + AI extraction of case details from notices and complaints
- **Court-Compliant Output:** Generated documents match court formatting requirements (Florida eviction, EEOC)
- **Deadline Management:** Automatic identification and tracking of critical filing deadlines
- **Multi-Domain Coverage:** Eviction defense + employment law in single platform

## Technical Highlights

- AI-powered document analysis (entity recognition, issue classification)
- Dynamic questionnaire generation based on legal issue type
- Court-compliant document templates with proper citations
- Automated deadline extraction from legal documents
- Settlement estimation algorithms
- Multi-language support (Spanish for eviction defense)

## System Capabilities

- **Eviction Defense:**
  - Answer to Complaint with affirmative defenses
  - Motion to Dismiss for defective notice
  - Habitability demand letters with photo evidence
  - Emergency motions to stay eviction

- **Employment Law:**
  - EEOC discrimination charges
  - Demand letters with settlement calculations
  - Wage & hour violation analysis
  - Retaliation timeline builders
