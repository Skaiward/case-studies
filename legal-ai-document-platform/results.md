## Lessons Learned

**What Worked:**
- AI excels at entity extraction from structured legal documents
- Users need visual deadline tracking, not just calendar reminders
- Plain language explanations critical for self-represented litigants
- Mobile-first design essential for target demographic
- Photo evidence integration valuable for habitability cases

**What We'd Do Differently:**
- Implement attorney review queue earlier for complex cases
- Add chat support for user questions during form completion
- Create video walkthroughs for each document type
- Build community forum for user-shared experiences
- Expand to more legal domains (family law, small claims)

**Production Gotchas:**
- Legal disclaimers must be prominent and repeated
- AI confidence thresholds need careful tuning (false positives costly)
- Court formatting rules vary even within same jurisdiction
- Users often upload poor quality photos (OCR quality issues)
- Deadline calculations complex with court holidays and weekends
